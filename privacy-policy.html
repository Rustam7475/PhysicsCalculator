Privacy Policy for PhysicsCalculator

Last updated: 2026-03-28

PhysicsCalculator respects your privacy. This app is designed to work primarily on your device and does not require account creation.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data such as name, email address, phone number, location, or contacts.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

  • Calculation history and favorites (saved locally using Core Data).
  • App settings such as selected language and theme (saved locally using UserDefaults).

This information remains on your device unless you remove the app or clear the stored data.

3. Analytics and Tracking

PhysicsCalculator does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

5. PDF Export and Sharing

If you choose to export or share a calculation result as PDF, the file is created on your device and shared only through actions you initiate.

6. Children

The app contains educational content and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in future versions. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
